Formula Library

GEOMETRY
Rectangle: Area = l×b, Perimeter = 2(l+b)
Square: Area = a², Perimeter = 4a
Triangle: Area = √3/4 a², Perimeter = 3a
Circle: Area = πr², Circumference = 2πr
Semi Circle: Area = ½πr², Perimeter = πr + 2r

VOLUME & SURFACE AREA
Cube: V = a³, TSA = 6a²
Cuboid: V = lbh, TSA = 2(lb+bh+lh)
Sphere: V = 4/3πr³, SA = 4πr²
Hemisphere: V = 2/3πr³, CSA = 2πr², TSA = 3πr²
Cylinder: V = πr²h, CSA = 2πrh, TSA = 2πr(h+r)
Hollow Cylinder: V = πh(R²-r²), CSA = 2πh(R+r)
Cone: V = 1/3πr²h, CSA = πrl, TSA = πr(l+r)

BMI = weight / height²
Speed = distance / time
