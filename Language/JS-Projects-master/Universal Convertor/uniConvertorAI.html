Unit Conversion 
Lenght :
1 mile = 1.609km  | 1 KM = 1000m
1 m = 100cm       | 1 ft = 12in
1 in = 2.54cm     | 1 cm = 10mm

Weight :
1 tonne = 1000 kg  | 1 kg = 2.204 lb
1 kg = 1000 g     | 1 g = 1000 mg

Temperature :
0°C = 273 K  |  0°C = 32 F
°F = (°C × 9/5) + 32
K = °C + 273.15

Time :
1 year = 12 months = 52 weeks = 365 days
1 month = 4 weeks=30/31 days
1 week = 7 days
1 day = 24 hr
1 hr = 60 min
1 min = 60 sec

GEOMETRY
Area :
Rectangle = l×b
Square = a²
Triangle = √s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c) where s = (a+b+c)/2
Right Angled Triangle = ½ (base x height)
Equlateral Triangle= √3/4 a²
Circle = πr²
Semi Circle = ½πr²
Parallelogram = b x h
Rhombus =  ½(d1 x d2) 
Trapezium = ½ (a+b) x h

Perimeter :
Rectangle = 2(l+b)
Square = Rhombus = 4a
Parallelogram = 2(base + slantside)
Triangle = a+b+c
Equlateral Triangle = 3a
Circle Circumference = 2πr
Semi Circle = πr + 2r
Trapezium = a+b+c+d 

VOLUME 
Cube: V = a³
Cuboid: V = lbh,
Sphere: V = 4/3πr³
Hemisphere: V = 2/3πr³
Cylinder: V = πr²h
Hollow Cylinder: V = πh(R²-r²)
Cone: V = 1/3πr²h

SURFACE AREA 
Cuboid:     LSA = 2(lb+bh)  TSA = 2(lb+bh+lh)
Cube:       LSA = 4a²  TSA = 6a²
Cone:       CSA = πrl  TSA = πr(l+r)
Sphere:     CSA = 4πr² TSA = 4πr²
Cylinder:   CSA = 2πrh TSA = 2πr(h+r)
Hemisphere: CSA = 2πr² TSA = 3πr²
Hollow Cylinder: CSA = 2πh(R+r)


BMI = weight (kg) / height²(in m)
Speed = distance / time
